1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

{{ app_name }} uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

CookieTypePurposeDuration

3. Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality including authentication, security, and session management. These cannot be disabled.

Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences such as theme settings and dashboard layout. These enhance your experience but are not strictly necessary.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform. We use this data to improve features and performance. All analytics data is anonymized.

4. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser Local Storage for:

  • Authentication tokens: JWT tokens for API authentication
  • UI preferences: Sidebar state, theme settings

Local Storage data remains until explicitly cleared by you or upon logout.

5. Third-Party Cookies

{{ app_name }} does not currently use third-party cookies. If we integrate third-party services in the future (analytics, support chat), this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from using the platform.
