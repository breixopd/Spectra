Spectra ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use {{ app_name }} ("Service").

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account data: Username, email address, and hashed password
  • Profile data: Display name, organization, role preferences
  • Assessment data: Target configurations, scan results, vulnerability findings
  • Support data: Communications with our support team

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Feature usage, session duration, pages visited
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Log data: IP address, access timestamps, error logs

  • Processing and storing security assessment results
  • Generating reports and analytics
  • Sending service-related notifications
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Cloud hosting, email delivery, payment processing (under data processing agreements)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services:

  • Account data: Until account deletion + 30 days
  • Assessment data: Per your plan retention period (default 90 days after mission completion)
  • Log data: 12 months
  • Billing records: As required by tax law (typically 7 years)

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request restricted processing of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@spectra.security. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security audits
  • Sandboxed execution environments for security tools

10. International Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the EEA, we ensure adequate protection through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other approved transfer mechanisms.

11. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

12. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Automated Decision-Making & Profiling

In accordance with GDPR Article 22, we provide the following information about automated processing:

  • AI-assisted analysis: The platform uses artificial intelligence to analyze security assessments, select appropriate testing tools, prioritize vulnerabilities, and generate findings reports.
  • No solely automated legal decisions: No decisions that produce legal effects or similarly significant effects on you are made solely through automated means. All AI-generated security findings are advisory in nature.
  • Human review: You may request human review of any AI-generated security finding or assessment result by contacting privacy@spectra.security.
  • Training data opt-in: Your assessment data is only used to improve our AI models if you explicitly enable the share_training_data preference in your account settings. This is disabled by default.
  • Profiling: We do not build behavioral profiles for advertising or marketing purposes. The only profiling performed is within security assessments you initiate, which is core to the service contract.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
